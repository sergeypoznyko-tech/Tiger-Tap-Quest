Quick troubleshooting

The app doesn’t open / crashes

Restart the app and your device. Make sure you have the latest version installed.

Audio or vibration is not working

Check your device volume, Do Not Disturb mode, and app permission settings.

Feature request or feedback

Send your suggestion to the support email. Feedback is reviewed and used to prioritize improvements.

Privacy note: The app does not collect user data. Emails are used only to reply to your request.
