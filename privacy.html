Privacy Policy

App: Tiger Coin Quest Developer: Konstanta TOV Contact: sergeypoznyko@gmail.com

Summary: Tiger Coin Quest may use Firebase Analytics and AppsFlyer. These services may collect and process limited usage data, app activity, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, and related technical information for analytics, attribution, app improvement, marketing measurement, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

1. Information we collect

Tiger Coin Quest may collect limited non-personal and technical data through third-party analytics and attribution services, including Firebase Analytics and AppsFlyer.

  • App activity and usage data: app launches, screen views, sessions, session duration, interactions with app features, and in-app events.
  • Device or other identifiers: advertising identifiers, app instance identifiers, device identifiers, or similar technical identifiers.
  • Approximate location: approximate location may be derived from technical information such as IP address for analytics, attribution, fraud prevention, regional reporting, and marketing measurement.
  • Technical data: device type, operating system version, app version, language, country or region, and diagnostic information.

2. Information we do not intentionally collect

We do not intentionally collect directly identifying personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, payment information, or contact information through the app.

3. How we use information

Collected data may be used for analytics, measuring app usage, understanding how users interact with the app, improving performance and stability, attribution and measurement of advertising or marketing campaigns, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

4. Information storage

We do not store user data on our own developer-controlled servers. However, data collected through Firebase Analytics and AppsFlyer may be stored and processed by those third-party service providers according to their own policies, retention rules, and applicable legal requirements.

If the app uses local device storage for basic functionality, such information is intended to remain on your device unless the app interface or operating system clearly states otherwise.

5. Information sharing and third-party services

Tiger Coin Quest may use third-party services including Firebase Analytics by Google and AppsFlyer. These services may collect, receive, process, and store usage data, app activity data, device identifiers, approximate location, and technical data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Usage data, app activity data, device identifiers, approximate location, and technical data may be shared with or processed by third-party analytics, attribution, advertising, marketing measurement, and fraud prevention providers.

We do not sell personal information. However, certain data may be processed by third-party service providers as described in this Privacy Policy.

6. Data security

Data transmitted from the app to third-party services is encrypted in transit using secure protocols such as HTTPS/TLS. Although reasonable security measures are used, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

7. User data deletion

At this time, Tiger Coin Quest does not provide a direct in-app mechanism or separate request process for users to request deletion of analytics, attribution, or device identifier data.

Accordingly, in the Google Play Data Safety section, the answer to whether users can request data deletion is: No.

8. Children’s privacy

Tiger Coin Quest is not intended to knowingly collect directly identifying personal information from children. The app may collect limited usage data, app activity data, device identifiers, approximate location, and technical data through third-party analytics and attribution services as described above.

9. Policy updates

If we change the app in a way that impacts privacy, we will update this page and keep the policy easy to understand.
