Privacy Policy

App: Tiger Tap Quest Developer: Konstanta TOV Contact: sergeypoznyko@gmail.com

Summary: Tiger Tap Quest does not collect any user data. We do not store data and we do not share data with anyone.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect personal information, sensitive information, or device identifiers. The app is designed to function without any data collection.

  • No account creation.
  • No analytics.
  • No advertising ID collection.
  • No background tracking.

2. Information storage

We do not store your personal information on servers because we do not collect it. If the app uses local device storage for basic functionality (for example, saving preferences), it remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

3. Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties because we do not collect user data.

4. Third-party services

Tiger Tap Quest does not integrate third-party SDKs that collect user data (such as analytics or ads). If this changes in the future, we will update this policy clearly and before enabling any data collection.

5. Policy updates

If we ever change the app in a way that impacts privacy, we will update this page and keep the policy easy to understand.
